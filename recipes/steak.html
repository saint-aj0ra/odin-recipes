Grilled Ribye Steak

get your carnivore on

this steak is out of control. unleash your inner beast with this extra thicc and juicy ribeye steak.

while it seems simple, simplicity is key for a great steak.

  • charcoal grill
  • charcoal
  • high quality (lots of marbling) ribeye steaks
  • lowry's seasoning salt
  • fresh cracked black pepper
  1. lay out your steaks on a plate or cookie sheet.
  2. sprinkle liberally with seasoned salt. leave out of fridge.
  3. heat up that charcoal and grill, son. about 525 degrees.
  4. when grill is hot (15 minutes or more, no less!) clean that bad boy.
  5. grill on first side 4 minutes. flip. grill second side 3 minutes.
  6. remove from grill. let rest at least 10 minutes before serving.
  7. EAT THAT THING, DOOD
