Roast Beast Sammy

get your carnivore on

this sammy is out of control. unleash your inner beast with this extra thicc roast beef and ham sammy.

i include tomato and djion mustard to give it a kick... you can add rabbit food if you like. no thanks.

  • decent quality bread
  • thinly sliced roast beef
  • thinly sliced smoked ham
  • garden fresh tomato
  • grey poupon dijon mustard
  1. slice up that bread, son. not too thick, not too thin.
  2. slice up those tomatos, son. enough to cover the bread in an even layer.
  3. lay down your roast beef. it should be a thicc boi - i prefer 1/2" of roast beef.
  4. add a single or double layer of smoked ham
  5. add a thin layer of grey poupon. the affordable, finer thing in life.
  6. EAT THAT THING, DOOD
